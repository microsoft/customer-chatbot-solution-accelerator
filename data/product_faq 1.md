## 1. What is the warranty on your products?
All electronics come with a 1-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects.

## 2. How do I track my order?
You can track your order using the tracking number provided in your shipment confirmation email.

## 3. Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we offer worldwide shipping. Shipping fees and delivery times vary by location.

## 4. Are your products compatible with Mac and Windows?
Most of our electronics are compatible with both Mac and Windows systems. Check the product specifications for details.

## 5. Can I cancel my order?
Orders can be canceled before they are shipped. Once shipped, you will need to follow the return policy.
